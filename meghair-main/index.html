Meet Meghan

I'm a 17-year hair veteran from western Maine, first learning the fundamentals of hair in Boston on the reputable Newbury Street. I then returned to my home state for 10 years where I was fortunate enough to build steadfast relationships with my clients.

My approach to hairstyling is deeply influenced by the Vidal Sassoon philosophy of precision cutting and personalized style. Every cut is a collaboration between stylist and client, resulting in a look that's both beautiful and manageable.
